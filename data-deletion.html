Data Deletion Instructions for NailIt

How to Delete Your Data

Option 1: Through the App

  1. Open the NailIt app
  2. Go to Profile Settings
  3. Scroll down and tap on "Delete Account"
  4. Follow the confirmation steps

Option 2: Via Email Request

Send an email to mohangir.sthakur@gmail.com with:

  • Subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Your registered email address
  • Your full name

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon your request, we will delete:

  • Your account information
  • Profile data
  • Usage history
  • Any stored preferences

Note: Some information may be retained for legal, security, or business requirements in accordance with our Privacy Policy and applicable laws.

Processing Time

We will process your deletion request within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the data deletion process, please contact us at mohangir.sthakur@gmail.com
